Common Leaf Diseases

Learn about different types of crop diseases we can detect

🍂
Leaf Spot Diseases
Fungal or bacterial infections causing spots on leaves. Common in tomatoes, peppers, and leafy greens.
Symptoms:
Brown/black spots, yellow halos, leaf drop
🦠
Blight
Rapid spread of disease causing wilting and death of plant tissue. Affects tomatoes, potatoes, and corn.
Symptoms:
Brown lesions, rapid wilting, stem cankers
🍃
Powdery Mildew
White powdery fungal growth on leaf surfaces. Common in cucumbers, squash, and grapes.
Symptoms:
White powdery coating, leaf curling, stunted growth
🌾
Rust
Fungal disease producing rust-colored spores. Affects wheat, beans, and roses.
Symptoms:
Orange/brown pustules, yellowing leaves, defoliation
🥀
Wilt Diseases
Vascular diseases causing plants to wilt and die. Includes Fusarium and Verticillium wilt.
Symptoms:
Yellowing, wilting, vascular discoloration
🐛
Pest Damage
Damage caused by insects and pests including aphids, caterpillars, and mites.
Symptoms:
Holes in leaves, chewed edges, sticky residue
